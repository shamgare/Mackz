How much is the delivery fee?

All orders and also delivery destinations are free of charge at all.

How long is the delivery time?

Your order will be dispatched within 2-5 working days (Indonesia Area) or 4-14 days for other areas depending on your address.

Can Products be sent out of Indonesia?

Our Online and Offline Store can ship out of Indonesia.

What if i can't receive the product at the time of delivery?

If you are unable to receive the product by the time the delivery is made, our delivery team will leave a message or contact you, and make a maximum of two delivery attempts. Re-delivery will be made on the next business day
